ABOUT ME

My name is Farrah. I live just outside of Fredericton NB; although I called Calgary AB my home for a good part of my life. I am a Market Manager for a popular paint manufacturing company - having been in the industry for too many years. I decided to go back to doing something that I loved as a young adult and that is coding! I am excited to say that I'm a Full Stack Web Development Student taking part in a Coding Bootcamp hosted by the University of New Brunwsick.

My hobbies include anything outdoors and learning ways to be self-sustainable. I also enjoy things related to design. I've been selling my crochet pattern designs on Etsy since 2009 and also own and manage an online clothing store.

To sum things up, I am a coder from the past learning new skills and I can't wait to put that knowledge to use. And that is exciting!
